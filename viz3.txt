We see a timeline of the most popular names per year, separated by gender. This way, we can see tendencies for the two genders and how they change over time.

Pros:
    - Easy to read and understand.
    - We can see all the most popular names over time at once.
    - We can see how long a name has been popular for.
Cons:
    - Comparison between gender only in the temporal dimension.
Potential ameliorations:
    - Possibility to filter by region.
